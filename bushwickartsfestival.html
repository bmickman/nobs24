ABOUT THE PROJECT

The Bushwick Arts Festival was a nonprofit organization that held an art event in 2016 as well as 2017. The organization was made up of artists from New York City as well as around the world. The event gained notoriety in a variety of local media outlets. Notorious Octopus worked closely with a array of diverse community members, LGBT creative leaders, artists, and business owners to successfully bring the event to fruition.

The Bushwick Arts Festival required branding and digital assets built from the ground up. There was a long list of digital work required, including a mobile-friendly website that could also display details of the program for the event. Notorious Octopus interfaced with a variety of local businesses and galleries to complete the project. Additionally, there were a variety of promotional materials, social media assets, programs, and logos that were produced. The website went through multiple iterations for 2016 and 2017 event programs, as well as the current informational website.
